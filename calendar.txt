13 Month Roman Solar Calendar:

I - Aprilis, 1st - Spring Equinox
II - Maius
III - Iunius
IV - Quadtilis, 7th - Summer Solstice
V - Quintilis
VI - Sextilis, **** Every 4 Years Between The 14th & 15th Leap Day Occurs ****
VII - September, 14th - Fall/Autumn Equinox
VIII - October
IX - November
X - December, 21st - Winter Solstice
XI - Undecember
XII - Ianuarius
XIII - Februarius
- - The Day Out of Time/Renewal/"New Years Eve" - -

- Each Month Has 28 Days (364 Days)
- Years Starts On The Spring Equinox, Aprilis 1st, Sunday
- The Day Out of Time, between Februarius 28th and Aprilis 1st
**** Extra Day "Leap Year, Every 4 Years" Would Lay Between Sextilis 14th & 15th, 366 Days (Only if year is divisible by 4. Unless divisible by 100, skip it. Unless divisible by 400, keep it.) ****
